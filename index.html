Requirements

All accounts in the top 300 are required to meet the kill/dead minimum requirements to stay in the KD. Top 300 in power are subject to the requirements. This includes alts/farms. DKP = T4 kills x1, + T5 kills x 5.25, + T4 deaths x 5, + T5 deaths x 10
